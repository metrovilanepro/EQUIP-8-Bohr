For the second species, meet...

Halococcus salifodinae

This micro-organism belongs to the Kingdom Archaea and lives in places with high-salt content like salt lakes and mines. It is able to survive there because it has special parts in its cells that protect it from drying out. Its reddish color can even change the color of the water or salt around it. Scientists study it to learn how life can survive in extreme environments, possibly on other planets.
