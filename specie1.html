Pyrocystis fusiformis

This is a tiny, single-celled organism from the Kingdom Protista that lives in the ocean. It can make its own food using sunlight (photosynthesis) and is known for bioluminescence, producing a blue-green light when the water is moved. This glow might help scare or confuse predators. They are also important in the ocean food chain as food for small marine animals.
